Future Dollar Calculator

The Cassandra handbag by Saint Laurent retails for $2,850. If that amount was placed in an investment vehicle today, what could its future value be?

  • We will assume an 8% average return per year
  • Interest compounded yearly
  • The average return of the S&P 500 was 10.7% from 1971-2020

About this website:

This is a project to increase financial literacy. There is nothing wrong with buying a nice handbag. The purpose of this application is to look at the full scope of some of our financial decisions, so that we can make the best choices for ourselves and our families.
